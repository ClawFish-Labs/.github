## About

ClawFish Labs is an AI research and engineering organization focused on creating intelligent agent systems that are practical, trustworthy, and deeply useful.

We believe AI should feel less like software and more like a capable partner — able to reason through problems, operate across tools, and help people create, learn, and build faster.

Our work explores:

* Autonomous AI agents
* Tool-using language models
* Multi-agent collaboration systems
* Human-centered AI experiences
* Open research and experimentation

---

## Philosophy

We are inspired by the idea that powerful AI should be:

* **Helpful** — designed to genuinely improve human capability
* **Reliable** — consistent, understandable, and safe to use
* **Curious** — capable of exploration and creative reasoning
* **Accessible** — open to builders, researchers, and communities everywhere

Technology should amplify human creativity, not replace it.

---

## What We're Building

ClawFish Labs is building the next generation of AI agents:

* Systems that can plan and execute complex tasks
* Agents that understand context over long interactions
* Tool-integrated workflows for developers and creators
* Research-driven infrastructure for scalable intelligence

Our long-term goal is to create AI systems that can collaborate naturally with humans across work, learning, and everyday life.

---

## Open Source

We believe open collaboration accelerates innovation.

This organization hosts experiments, frameworks, internal tooling, and public research projects related to intelligent agents and modern AI systems.

As the ecosystem evolves, ClawFish Labs aims to contribute tools and ideas that help shape a more capable and responsible AI future.
